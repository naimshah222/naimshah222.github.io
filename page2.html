FORMAL CORRESPONDENCES

What is Formal
Correspondences?

  1. Written communication by exchanging letters
  2. Consists of memos, letters, and electronic mail.

Why it is important?

  1. An effective way to make requests, submit changes to a job, and deliver specific information.
  2. Unlike telephone conversations, correspondence presents the audience with a legal contract that is dated and can support a claim in court.

Letters of Orders

  1. A letter of order is a document that confirms the details of a purchase of goods or services from one party to another.
  2. It usually includes more information about what you are ordering, like quantity, model number, or color, the payment terms, and the matter in which the products are to be shipped.
  3. When the recipient receives this letter, they will process the order and send the merchandise.

Letters of Enquiries

  1. Why do we write a letter of enquiry?  - You probably found the answers to some of your questions, but not to all of them. So, how can you find out the information you need? The answer is simple. Write a letter (or email) of enquiry!
